What is
Sigma?

Purpose

Sigma is first and foremost an event for the SUTD family to come together to share and celebrate their collective summer experiences. It is an opportunity for students to showcase all the work they've done over the summer, for companies to meet and interact with prospective students, and most importantly for people to learn from one another and to build meaningful connections.

Structure

Sigma is a two-day event. The first day will be onsite at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), and the second day would be offsite at a more central location. This structure is designed to encourage participation from members outside of our immediate community, increasing opportunities to network and grow.

Lightning Talks

Several lightning talks will be delivered by selected individuals during the course of the event. There will be up to three talks held concurrently, and each talk will last around 10 minutes + 5 minutes for questions/overtime. The aim of these talks is to highlight outstanding projects and works that were undertaken by members of the SUTD community. All talks will be recorded and uploaded at a later date.
